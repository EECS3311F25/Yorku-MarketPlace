**Project Overview:**  
York Marketplace is a secure buy/sell platform designed exclusively for York University students and professors.  
Users can post, browse, and trade items safely within the campus community using verified YorkU Email.

---


### **User Story 1: Account Registration**
**As a** York community member (student or professor),  
**I want** to create a new account using my YorkU email  
**so that** I can securely access the marketplace.  

**Criteria of Satisfaction (CoS):**  
- Registration form includes name, email, and password.  
- System only accepts valid `@yorku.ca` or `@my.yorku.ca` emails.  
- Unverified accounts cannot log in.  

---

### **User Story 2: Email Verification**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to verify my account through an email confirmation link  
**so that** only legitimate York users can participate.  

**CoS:**  
- Verification email sent automatically after registration.  
- Clicking the link activates the account.  
- Verified status is shown on profile.  

---

### **User Story 3: User Login**
**As a** registered user,  
**I want** to log into my account  
**so that** I can access my personalized dashboard.  

**CoS:**  
- Login form validates credentials.  
- Incorrect attempts show error messages.  
- Successful login redirects to dashboard.  

---

### **User Story 4: Password Reset**
**As a** user who forgot my password,  
**I want** to reset it via my YorkU email  
**so that** I can regain access to my account.  

**CoS:**  
- “Forgot Password” link sends a reset email.  
- New password takes effect immediately.  

---

### **User Story 5: Create a Listing**
**As a** verified user,  
**I want** to create a new item listing  
**so that** I can sell items to others at York.  

**CoS:**  
- User can upload item title, description, category, price, and images.  
- Listing preview appears before submission.  
- Successful creation confirms with a message.  

---

### **User Story 6: Browse Listings**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to browse all available listings  
**so that** I can discover items for sale on campus.  

**CoS:**  
- Listings displayed with images, titles, prices, and seller info.  
- Pagination or scroll loads more results.  

---

### **User Story 7: Search for Items**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to search for items by keyword or category  
**so that** I can quickly find what I’m looking for.  

**CoS:**  
- Search bar filters listings dynamically.  
- Supports category and price filters.  

---

### **User Story 8: View Listing Details**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to view detailed information about an item  
**so that** I can decide whether to contact the seller.  

**CoS:**  
- Displays seller name, price, description, and contact button.  
- Item condition and upload date visible.  

---

### **User Story 9: Contact Seller**
**As a** buyer,  
**I want** to send a message to the seller within the app  
**so that** I can discuss the item without sharing personal contact info.  

**CoS:**  
- Message form sends notifications to seller.  
- Chat history stored in user inbox.  

---

### **User Story 10: Chat Notifications**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to receive in-app or email notifications  
**so that** I know when someone messages me.  

**CoS:**  
- Notification bell shows unread message count.  
- Optional email alerts for new chats.  

---

### **User Story 11: Edit or Delete Listing**
**As a** seller,  
**I want** to modify or remove my item listings  
**so that** I can update prices or delete sold items.  

**CoS:**  
- Seller can edit title, description, or price.  
- Delete option permanently removes listing.  

---

### **User Story 12: Buyer/Seller Filters**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to filter listings by “student-only” or “professor-only” categories  
**so that** I can choose whom to trade with.  

**CoS:**  
- Each listing includes a “visibility” option (students/professors/both).  
- Buyers can apply filter on the marketplace page.  

---

### **User Story 13: Profile Page**
**As a** user,  
**I want** a profile page showing my details and listings  
**so that** others can view my credibility.  

**CoS:**  
- Displays user name, role (student/professor), verified status.  
- Shows all active listings.  

---

### **User Story 14: Rating System**
**As a** buyer or seller,  
**I want** to rate my transaction partner  
**so that** trust within the marketplace improves.  

**CoS:**  
- 1–5 star rating after each transaction.  
- Average rating displayed on profiles.  

---

### **User Story 15: Report Listing or User**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to report inappropriate listings or behavior  
**so that** the marketplace remains safe.  

**CoS:**  
- Report button on every listing.  
- Admin receives detailed report automatically.  

---

### **User Story 16: Admin Dashboard**
**As an** administrator,  
**I want** to monitor listings and users  
**so that** I can remove inappropriate content or ban users.  

**CoS:**  
- Admin view includes all active listings.  
- Ability to suspend users or remove posts.  

---

### **User Story 17: Transaction Confirmation**
**As a** buyer,  
**I want** to mark a purchase as “completed”  
**so that** the item listing can be closed and rated.  

**CoS:**  
- “Complete Transaction” button ends listing visibility.  
- Triggers post-transaction rating prompt.  

---

### **User Story 18: Saved Items**
**As a** user,  
**I want** to save or bookmark items  
**so that** I can revisit them later.  

**CoS:**  
- “Save” icon toggles saved state.  
- Saved items appear in user’s profile.  

---

### **User Story 19: Dark Mode**
**As a** frequent user,  
**I want** a dark mode option  
**so that** I can browse comfortably at night.  

**CoS:**  
- Toggle in settings switches between light and dark themes.  
- Theme preference is saved.  

---

### **User Story 20: Mobile Responsiveness**
**As a** user,  
**I want** the marketplace to work smoothly on mobile devices  
**so that** I can buy and sell conveniently from my phone.  

**CoS:**  
- Pages adjust automatically to screen size.  
- Images and buttons remain functional on mobile view.

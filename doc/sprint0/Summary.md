## **Project Summary — YorkU Marketplace**

### **Overview**

YorkU Marketplace is a secure online platform designed exclusively for the York University community. It allows verified users with a valid yorku.ca email to buy, sell, and exchange items and services within a trusted campus environment. The goal is to make transactions safer, faster, and more convenient for students, faculty, and staff while promoting sustainability and affordability across campus.

### **Project Objectives**

• Build a verified marketplace that only yorku.ca account holders can access.

• Support easy posting, browsing, and purchasing of items and services.

• Offer a private in-app messaging feature for communication without sharing personal contact details.

• Encourage sustainable practices by facilitating the exchange and reuse of textbooks, furniture, and supplies.

• Design a responsive and accessible interface that works on both desktop and mobile devices.

### **Key Users**

• Students who wish to buy or sell used goods and services safely within the York community.

• Faculty and staff who may offer office supplies, books, or tickets.

• Campus clubs and organizations looking to promote events, fundraisers, or ticket exchanges.

### **Key Use Cases**

• A student posts a textbook for sale with a price, photo, and course tag.

• Another student searches by course code, views listings, and messages the seller.

• A club lists discounted tickets for a York event and verified users purchase them through the platform.

• A staff member gives away office equipment and students claim it using the in-app reservation feature.

### **Key Usage Scenarios**

• **Scenario 1:** A student finishing a course lists an EECS 2030 textbook. Another student finds the listing, messages the seller, and meets on campus to complete the transaction.

• **Scenario 2:** The Robotics Club posts Winter Gala tickets. Students verify with their yorku.ca accounts, purchase tickets, and coordinate pickup through in-app chat.

• **Scenario 3:** A staff member posts two monitors for giveaway. Students following the “Electronics” category receive notifications and claim them.

### **Intended Audience**

• **Instructors and TAs:** This summary clearly explains the project’s concept, objectives, users, and key scenarios to allow for accurate evaluation.

• **Team Members:** The document will guide development priorities, user story creation, and sprint planning in future phases.

### **Summary**

YorkU Marketplace provides a trusted and verified environment for campus members to exchange goods and services safely. It emphasizes community connection, sustainability, and ease of use while giving the team a clear foundation for design and implementation in future sprints.
